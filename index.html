About

Hi, i'm Tomer!
In order to develop and design that site, I used mostly frontend technologies
(such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript) and a couple of external libraries (Bootstrap and jQuery).

The crypto market has been on the rise in the last few years,
and the need for tracking trends in that market inspired me to create a site,
a place where enthusiasts can get:

  • Currencies of dozens of virtual coins in the crypto market.
  • Tracking options for specific coins based on your preferences.
  • A responsive UI for all devices!
